Q: What is Oasis Microfinance?

  • A: Oasis Microfinance is a financial institution that provides small loans and other financial services to low-income individuals and small businesses.

    • Q: What types of loans do you offer?

  • A: We offer various loan products, including personal loans, business loans, and group loans.


    • Q: What are the loan terms and interest rates?

  • A: Our loan terms range from 6 months to 24 months, and our interest rates are competitive and affordable.

    • Q: How do I apply for a loan?

  • A: visit our office or contact our customer service to guide you through the application process.

    • Q: What are the requirements for a loan?

  • A: You need to be at least 18 years old, have a valid ID, proof of income, and a bank account.

    • Q: How long does it take to process a loan?

  • A: We process loans within 24-48 hours after receiving all required documents.

    • Q: Can I repay my loan early?

  • A: Yes, you can repay your loan early without any penalties or fees.

    • Q: Do you offer savings accounts?

  • A: Yes, we offer savings accounts with competitive interest rates and flexible terms.

    • Q: How do I access my account?

  • A: You can access your account online, through our mobile app, or by visiting our office.

    • Q: Is my personal information secure?

  • A: Yes, we take data privacy and security seriously, and our systems are encrypted to protect your information.

    • Q: What if I have a complaint or feedback?

  • A: You can contact our customer service department, and we will respond promptly to address your concerns.
