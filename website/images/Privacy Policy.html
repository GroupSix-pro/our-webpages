At Oasis Microfinance, we value the trust you place in us when you share your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect - Personal information: name, address, phone number, email, and identification documents. - Financial information: loan applications, payment history, and credit scores. - Transaction information: loan disbursals, repayments, and account activity.

How We Use Your Information - To process loan applications and manage your account. - To verify your identity and creditworthiness. - To communicate with you about your loan, account, or our services. - To comply with legal and regulatory requirements. - To improve our services and develop new products.

How We Protect Your Information - We use encryption to protect your data in transit. - We store your information securely on our servers. - We limit access to your information to authorized personnel. - We comply with data protection regulations and industry standards.

Data Sharing - We may share your information with: - Credit bureaus and credit reference agencies. - Our partners and service providers. - Law enforcement and regulatory agencies. - Third-party lenders or investors (with your consent).

Your Rights - Access and correct your personal information. - Request deletion or restriction of processing. - Object to processing for marketing or other purposes. - File a complaint with the relevant authorities.
